Mass Spectrometry Services

State-of-the-art proteomic and peptidomic analysis services for milk and dairy research.

Proteomic Analysis

Comprehensive protein analysis using advanced mass spectrometry techniques to identify, quantify, and characterize proteins in milk and dairy products.

Services Include:

  • Protein identification and quantification
  • Post-translational modification analysis
  • Protein-protein interaction studies
  • Comparative proteomics
  • Custom protein analysis protocols

Applications:

  • Milk protein composition analysis
  • Processing effects on protein structure
  • Quality control and standardization
  • Novel protein discovery

Peptidomic Analysis

Advanced peptide analysis to identify and characterize bioactive peptides in milk and dairy products, including their functional properties and digestion products.

Services Include:

  • Peptide identification and sequencing
  • Bioactive peptide discovery
  • Digestion product analysis
  • Peptide quantification
  • Custom peptide analysis protocols

Applications:

  • Bioactive peptide discovery
  • Digestion studies
  • Functional food development
  • Quality assessment

Our Equipment

We utilize state-of-the-art mass spectrometry equipment to provide high-quality analysis services.

Orbitrap Fusion Lumos

High-resolution mass spectrometer for comprehensive proteomic and peptidomic analysis.

  • Ultra-high resolution (up to 500,000 at m/z 200)
  • Advanced fragmentation capabilities (HCD, CID, ETD)
  • High sensitivity (low attomole level)
  • Advanced data acquisition modes
  • Real-time search capabilities
  • Comprehensive proteome coverage

Q Exactive HF-X

High-field Orbitrap mass spectrometer for rapid, high-throughput analysis.

  • Fast scanning capabilities
  • High mass accuracy
  • Excellent sensitivity

Nano-LC System

Advanced liquid chromatography system for optimal separation of complex samples.

  • High separation efficiency
  • Low flow rates
  • Excellent reproducibility

Data Analysis and Reporting

Comprehensive data analysis and reporting services to help you understand and utilize your results effectively.

Services Include:

  • Advanced data processing
  • Statistical analysis
  • Custom visualization
  • Detailed reporting
  • Data interpretation support
